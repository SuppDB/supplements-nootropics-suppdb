§ FAQ · Frequently Asked Questions

Technical Supplement & Nootropic Dataset FAQ

How are proprietary blends handled in the SuppDB dataset?

Every ingredient record features an explicit boolean flag (is_proprietary_blend). When a product lists a proprietary blend, the total blend mass is recorded, and constituent ingredients are linked under the shared blend hierarchy without fabricating unverified individual dosages.

Can I get NIH PubChem CIDs and InChIKeys with every supplement ingredient?

Yes! We cross-reference every normalized active botanical and chemical compound against NIH PubChem, enriching records with exact PubChem CIDs, molecular weights, canonical SMILES, and unique InChIKeys.

What format is the dataset delivered in when I purchase the snapshot?

The full dataset snapshot ($49) is delivered immediately via email as a clean, relational SQLite database (suppdb.sqlite) alongside normalized CSV tables (products.csv, ingredients.csv, and brands.csv) and JSON dumps.

Can I download a free sample before licensing the full 17,000+ product dataset?

Absolutely. You can download our verified 2,250+ row open sample CSV (samples/suppdb_sample.csv) directly from our landing page or GitHub repository under a Creative Commons license.
